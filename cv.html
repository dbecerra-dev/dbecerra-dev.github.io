Diego Becerra
Universidad de Concepción

diegobecerra@udec.cl

I am a Ph.D. candidate advised by Professor Harvey Zambrano at the Chemical Engineering Department, Universidad de Concepción.
My research interest mainly fall into two areas:
(i) Active research on advanced computational simulations of micro and nanoscale fluid flows
(ii) Rheological study of complex fluids.
I am experienced in conducting atomistic and stochastic simulations of complex fluids in serial and parallel codes.
